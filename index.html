Question 4 of 7
Do you know how much investment income your corporation earns each year?
Here is the rule most business owners don't know: once your corporation earns more than $50,000 a year from investments, the CRA starts cutting your small business tax break. For every dollar over $50,000 — you lose $5 of that tax break. At $150,000 — it's completely gone.
Canada.ca — Federal Budget 2018, Passive Income Business Limit Reduction. In effect since the 2019 tax year.
